Hello, welcome to the Baptium_template V1, this template is free and the development of this website is progressive. I am a French student, I am not an expert.

This is planned to soon add a language selection menu and for users to choose between light or dark theme. We are dedicated to improving the user experience and functionality of the template. Your feedback is valuable to us in enhancing its features.
